We specialize in redesigning and enhancing existing MVPs to make them more scalable, user-friendly, and investor-ready. Our team will evaluate your current product and recommend the most efficient path forward.

We primarily work on a fee-for-service basis, but in select cases, we consider hybrid arrangements with a reduced fee plus equity. This is evaluated on a case-by-case basis during our initial consultation.
